What is Exam Platform?

Exam Platform is a service designed to assist educational organizations in creating and administering exams for their students with ease.


Features:

1. Examiners can create and deploy exams effortlessly.

2. Students receive the exam directly on their dashboard.

3. Each question in the exam is timed to prevent plagiarism.

All of these features and more are demonstrated in this tutorial video.


About:

This project was inspired by my personal experience. I was in my final year at university when the war in Sudan began on October 14th, 2023. As a result, many people, including myself, were displaced, making it challenging for the university to conduct exams for students scattered across different countries. This situation led to the development of the online exam platform idea.
